Refund policy

  • No Refunds for Missed Classes: If a student misses a class, no refunds will be provided. However, rescheduling is allowed with a 24-hour notice prior to the class.
  • Full Refund: If a student withdraws before the first class, a full refund will be issued.
  • Pro-Rated Refund: If a student withdraws after the first class or becomes unsatisfied, a pro-rated refund will be issued, minus a 10% administrative fee.
  • Cancellations by Instructor: If a class is canceled by the instructor, students will receive either a full refund or a free rescheduled class.
